## Idea
Modern healthcare is largely reactive, identifying diseases only after symptoms become severe, which often leads to delayed treatment, higher medical costs, and preventable emergencies. AUTRYST addresses this challenge by introducing an AI-powered Health Intelligence System that continuously analyzes health signals such as vital signs, sleep, nutrition, activity, and medical history to create a personalized AI Health Twin. Instead of relying on occasional hospital visits, the system detects unusual health patterns at an early stage, provides preventive recommendations, alerts family members during critical situations, and supports timely medical intervention. By enabling continuous monitoring and intelligent decision-making, AUTRYST empowers individuals to take control of their health before complications arise. Our vision is to make preventive healthcare accessible, affordable, and proactive, improving quality of life while reducing avoidable hospitalizations and easing the burden on healthcare systems worldwide.
